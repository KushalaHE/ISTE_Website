Who are we?

SSIT-ISTE Students' Chapter being established during the year 1991 with the motto "TRAINING IS EVERYTHING, WE CAN GET EXTRAORDINARY RESULTS OUT OF ORDINARY PEOPLE BY TRAINING AND TRAINING" is actively functioning by organizing events like: guest lectures by experts, technical workshops and trainings that help students to update their knowledge about the new technologies. ISTE Students' Chapter of the institution is organizing a National Level Technical Symposium - TANTRA. The team being guided by our Dean Student Affairs, Manjula Yerva, Dept. of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, SSIT, and Dean Academic Dr. Renukalatha. S Professor and HOD, Department of Computer science and engineering, SSIT, Tumakuru. The main phase of ISTE Students' Chapter in SSIT started Technical Fest TANTRA in the year 2008 and is conducted every year. The continued success and growth of the SSIT-ISTE Students' Chapter and its flagship event, TANTRA, speak volumes about the dedication and passion of its members and organizers. Over the years, TANTRA has evolved into a platform that not only showcases technical prowess but also encourages innovation, creativity, and teamwork among participants. The collaborative efforts of students, faculty members, in conceptualizing and executing TANTRA have contributed to its reputation as a premier technical symposium. Through a diverse array of competitions, workshops, mock interviews and keynote speeches, TANTRA has become a melting pot of ideas and experiences, enriching the learning journey of all involved.

(Falco) is an industry leader in designing and manufacturing magnetic-based electronic components for power conversion, energy metering, and telecommunications. The industrial visit on December 8, 2021, allowed 47 students from Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology to explore their manufacturing processes and product applications, accompanied by faculty members Mr. Puneeth K and Dr. Batluri Tilak Chandra.
